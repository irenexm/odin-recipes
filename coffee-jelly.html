Coffee Jelly

Description

A tasty, refreshing desert

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 5 mins
  • Cool and set: 5 hrs 15 mins
  • Total: 5 hrs 25 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unflavored gelatin powder
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 cups brewed strong black coffee
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Whipped cream, optional

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the gelatin powder and water.
  2. Place the brewed coffee and sugar in a medium saucepan and bring to a near boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Turn off the heat, and whisk in the gelatin mixture until it dissolves. Set aside to cool, about 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Pour the coffee gelatin mixture into a shallow baking dish (8- or 9-inch square or 11 x 7 inches) and refrigerate until set, about 5 hours.
  5. Once the coffee jelly has set, cut the gelatin into 1/2-inch cubes.
  6. Spoon the cubes into individual serving dishes and top with whipped cream, if desired.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 31 calories; protein 2g; carbohydrates 6g; fat 0g.
